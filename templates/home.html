Background

The original dataset had a set of 500 observations. While doing EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis), we found that some wind values were generating zero power. We figured that these observations were due to maintenance or fault and so we dropped them as outlier that may taint the generalisation ability of our model. In doing research into wind power generation, we found that there is a cut off for power generation in wind turbines where wind speeds can be too high and can damage the motor, hub and blades and so the upper limit of wind speed exist. The two models we have chosen are Linear Regression (excluding outliers) and Random Forest. Both models predict reasonably well the power generated from a given wind value however the random forest model has higher predictive power when dealing with lower and high values making it better generalized model.
